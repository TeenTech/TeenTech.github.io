TeenTech is an interactive tech community run by teens for teens. TeenTech NY and TeenTech SF are bi-coastal founding partners in the TeenTech GLOBAL network, bringing the spirit of Silicon Valley and Silicon Alley to the rest of the world. We have connected over 500 students with 81 tech mentors from Apple, Electronic Arts, Google, Khan Academy, LinkedIn, NASA, Oracle Team USA, and Zynga.
