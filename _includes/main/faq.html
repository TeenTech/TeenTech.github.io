FAQ

What is Teen Tech NY?

TeenTech NY is a community of like-minded individuals. Our goal is to open up the tech community to anyone and everyone. We do this by hosting events such as this falls Bicoastal Teen Tech conference where various experts in the tech industry lead workshops on technology.

Where will it be?

The event will be held at Microsoft’s offices.

Can my parents come?

Unfortunately, there won’t be enough room to bring your parents.

Who can attend?

TeenTech NY is open to New York City students 9th grade and up!

When is it?

October 18th 2014

How much will it cost?

It’s FREE for all high school students. Our goal is to make technology as accessible as possible so we don’t want cost to stop people from attending.

What if it rains?

Who cares? Tech knows no weather! Plus I hear Microsoft have super nice offices!
