Quiessence Phillips

Quiessence’s personal motto is “Polished Exterior, Techie Interior”. She is a Cyber Security professional with over 7 years working in the financial industry. Quiessence is also the Curriculum Development Lead for Black Girls Code NY, creator of the Girltechie Campaign, and a workshop series called Digital Dilemmas. She’s a mom, mentor, coder, hacker, strategist, and change agent.
