Samantha Zucker

Samantha Zucker is a User Experience Designer at Knewton. She aims to build products that solve real problems by making the complex simple and designing technology with empathy. She loves understanding how technology can fit into people's lives to make lasting, positive impact.

She utilizes a wide array of tools in her daily work, including user research, iteration, rapid prototyping and usability testing, and has been fortunate to apply her design thinking skill set in a number of mediums. Prior to Knewton, she has worked on iPhone and iPad apps, International Development research, animation, art installations and exhibit design.

Samantha attended Carnegie Mellon and graduated from the Interdisciplinary Program with a self designed BHA in Design and Linguistics.
