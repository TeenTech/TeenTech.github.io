Tamar Shapiro

Tamar Shapiro is Vice President of Analytics, Insights & Experimentation within the Digital Customer Experience team in American Express’ Technology organization. Her team analyzes the performance of the company website through advanced analytics and reporting. They listen to the voice of our customers and observe their behaviors on the site to identify pain points and other opportunities to improve the customer experience. They also perform experiments to optimize customers’ satisfaction and engagement with the site. These include usability tests, as well as A/B and multivariate testing methodologies. The team also manages capabilities for website tracking, tag management, customer insights and experimentation, plus data and reporting capabilities for the digital space.

Tamar began her career at American Express 10 years ago in the Risk and Information Management organization doing marketing analytics and decision science for acquisition campaigns to prospective customers. She began in the direct marketing channel and later moved into the digital space as that became the key channel for acquisitions. Prior to joining American Express, Tamar worked at Deloitte Consulting as a Senior Consultant in the Human Capital practice.

Tamar is a co-chair of the New York Analytics Collective, and has been a speaker at Girls Who Code. Past volunteer experience has included the Minds Matter organization, where she served as a mentor to high school students from low-income families.

Tamar holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics & Biometry from Cornell University and a Master of Science degree in Engineering from Columbia University.
