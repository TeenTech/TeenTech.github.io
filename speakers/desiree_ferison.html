Desiree Frieson

Desiree Frieson is the lead organizer of 3 Day Startup NYC, a nonprofit teaching entrepreneurial skills to students and young entrepreneurs. Over the last four years, she has worked with startups and digital media companies on marketing, outreach and public relation strategies. Desiree is also was once the founder of a media related startup. Desiree is passionate about all things technology, innovation, and entrepreneurial based education. She currently works at NeighborWorks America as the associate public affairs and communications advisor by day.
