About Us

TeenTech is an interactive community of like-minded individuals run by teens for teens. Headquartered in New York City, TeenTech NY and TeenTech SF are bi-coastal founding partners in the TeenTech GLOBAL network, bringing the spirit of Silicon Valley and Silicon Alley to the rest of the world. Our goal is to help high schoolers discover the possibilities of tech and promote diversity in the field. We do this by hosting various events throughout the year. Partnered with TeenTechSF, we have connected over 500 students with 81 tech mentors from organizations such as Apple, Cisco, EA, Google, Khan Academy, Indigogo, Microsoft, NASA, UBIsoft, West Point.

Technology has the power to change the world, we just have to realize its potential!
