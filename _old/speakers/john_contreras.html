John Contreras

John Contreras is a professor at Baruch College and Bramson Ort College in New York City. He attended The New York Institute of Technology and received his Masters at The University of San Francisco in 2002. He was discovered by a representative from Ubisoft while they visited one of his computer classes at San Francisco. "After an intense interview, I was hired as a storyboard artist and 3D modeler."

John has be working for Ubisoft for 14 years. He has been teaching at Baruch College for 4 years and working with programs such as 3ds Max and Final Cut Pro. He has also been teaching at Bramson Ort College for more than 2 years where he teaches Game Maker Studio, Unity, Maya, and Unreal Development 4. He also teach a storyboard class at Bramson.

Throughout his career, John received numerous awards such as best storyboard artist and character modeling from his graduate school. "I love teaching and being able to show my students my work samples and what it takes to become a game designer. I attend many field trips like The Museum of Moving Images in Queens, New York and visit the indie festival earlier the year."
